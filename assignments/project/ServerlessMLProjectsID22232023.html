KTH ID2223 Course Projects for 2022/3

This page showcases some of the “prediction services” developed by the students in the “Scalable ML and DL” masters level course. The main requirements for the project were to build a complete ML system that includes (1) a data source that is continually updated, and (2) solves a prediction problem. Most projects used some variant of the following architecture:

Showcase Projects

  1. Football Betting Predictions
  2. Bitcoin Price Prediction
  3. Ski Snow Depth Prediction
  4. Air Quality Predictions for Poland
  5. Stockholm house price predictions
  6. Song Recommender for Spotify Playlists
  7. Vocal Remover from music/sound files
  8. Stock Market Price Prediction using Sentiment Analysis
  9. TSLA stock price prediction
  10. Real-time traffic prediction near Kista
  11. Reddit - predict if a post will be liked or not
  12. Show news articles for a specified sentiment
  13. Water Flow Prediction for Stockholm Region
  14. Double the resolution of your image
  15. Electricity Price Prediction for Sweden (SE3)
  16. Name that Face - face recognition
  17. Aurora Prediction for Kiruna
